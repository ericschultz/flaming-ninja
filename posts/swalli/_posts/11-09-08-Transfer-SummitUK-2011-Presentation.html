I gave a presentation at the Transfer Summit/UK 2011 conference at Keble College, Oxford entitled "Symbian: collaboration, open, closed, dead". I present the history of the Symbian Foundation in the context of what foundations do and why it's important. The Transfer Summit/UK is in its second year, and it remains a great event. I would encourage keeping an eye out for it next year.
