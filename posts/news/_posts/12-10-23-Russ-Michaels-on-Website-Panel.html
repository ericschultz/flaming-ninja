Russ Michaels is an employee of Blue Thunder Internet which is a small hosting company. Prior to using Website Panel, Blue Thunder Internet used Helm. When asked about looking for a new program to use, Russ said he had noticed that similar programs on the market that do support Windows have so many issues and problems; he didn’t even want to go down that road again. Russ claims that Website Panel looked like the best bet because it was open source, adding that if they found themselves in a situation where the product didn’t do something they wanted it to, they could at least pay someone to do it. There is that flexibility with WSP.

While Russ does acknowledge that Website Panel was struggling on the support end, he realizes that there aren’t a lot of developers who know hot o make the changes his company needed. However, it’s only since Russ has been in touch with Omar that he has noticed Omar has taken charge and there has been a visible, big difference. According to Russ, Omar still does not have quite enough developers but it is a work in progress. If it wasn’t for Omar’s dedication to the project, Russ doubts that his company would have continue their use of the project.
