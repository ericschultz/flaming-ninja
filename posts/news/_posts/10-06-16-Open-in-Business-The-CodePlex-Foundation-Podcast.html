  Project Leader Garrett Serak provides an update on the CoApp Project and what's changed since it was contributed to the SIIG Gallery of the CodePlex Foundation in April 2010.

The CoApp project involves the development of new tools, library repositories, management systems and guidance that support interoperability by providing open source applications with support for features and benefits found on Windows.

Listen to Garrett on our inaugural podcast.
