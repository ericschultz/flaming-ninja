News from Microsoft Research, the Outercurve ChronoZoom project was awarded "Top Educational Resource at SXSW Interactive last night (March 12, 2013).

Our congratulations to the entire team of software engineers, program managers, and project leaders at Microsoft Research Connections in Redmond, Washington, and students and professors at Moscow State University in Russia and at UC Berkeley and University of Washington in the United States.
