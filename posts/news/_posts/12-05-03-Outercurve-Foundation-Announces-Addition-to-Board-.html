--- title: "Outercurve Foundation Announces Addition to Board of Directors" alias: /News/articleType/ArticleView/articleId/55/Outercurve-Foundation-Announces-Addition-to-Board-of-Directors tags: - Press Releases --- Wakefield, Mass., May 3, 2012 -The Outercurve Foundation today announced that Jim Jagielski of Red Hat and the Apache Software Foundation was elected to a second term on its Board of Directors. Gianugo Rabellino of Microsoft Open Technologies will also serve, replacing original Microsoft board member Stephanie Davies Boesch.

The two board members bring deep expertise and experience, and will help Outercurve in its mission to enable the exchange of code and understanding among software companies and open source communities.

Jim Jagielski is a well known and acknowledged expert and visionary in open source and IT, an accomplished coder (in numerous languages) and frequent presenter/interviewee/consultant on Web and Cloud related topics. He is best known as one of the developers and co-founders of the Apache Software Foundation, where he serves as President. Jagielski works for Red Hat as Senior Consulting Software Engineer in the office of the CTO, and is also a director of the Open Source Initiative (OSI).

Gianugo Rabellino currently serves as Senior Director of Open Source Communities at Microsoft Open Technologies. He is also a Vice President of the Apache XML Project Management Committee and Founder and former Chief Executive Officer of Sourcesense. Rabelino has a deep understanding of open source technologies and platforms. He brings a wealth of experience and knowledge to the Board, where he will advocate for interoperability and openness between Microsoft and non-Microsoft platforms.

“As a young organization, we have accomplished a great deal and have been successful building a diverse OSS community,” said Paula Hunter, Executive Director, Outercurve Foundation. “Jim and Gianugo’s board appointments enhance the Board’s expertise and diversity, and will help the foundation participate in and collaborate with the dynamic world of open source.”

About The Outercurve Foundation
The Outercurve Foundation is a non-profit foundation providing software IP management and project development governance to enable and encourage organizations to develop software collaboratively in open source communities for faster results. The Outercurve Foundation is the only open source foundation that is platform, technology, and license agnostic. For more information about the Outercurve Foundation contact info@Outercurve.org.
